Publishing Your Book

Who can publish on Volant Reads?

Anyone can publish on Volant Reads! Volant Reads is a literary marketplace that empowers independent authors by providing a platform to sell their works while retaining ownership and receiving the majority of sales revenue. You do not need to be a member of Volant Reads. Poets, novelists, non-fiction writers, and authors of all genres are welcome.

How much do authors earn?

Authors receive 90% of net revenue from sales of their works. Net revenue is calculated after payment processing fees, taxes, refunds, and chargebacks. Volant Reads retains 10% to cover platform and processing costs.

Do I keep my copyright?

Yes! Authors retain full ownership and copyright of their works. Volant Reads is a literary marketplace that empowers independent authors by providing a platform to sell their works while retaining ownership and receiving the majority of sales revenue. You grant Volant Reads permission to display and sell your book, but the rights remain yours.

Who promotes my book?

Authors are responsible for promoting their books. We provide you with a store link and book page link that you can share with your audience. Promote your book on social media, your website, email newsletters, and through your personal networks to drive sales.

How do I track my sales?

Sales are recorded in real time. You can track your earnings instantly through your author dashboard. Monitor your sales, view your earnings, and see your performance at a glance.

When are authors paid?

Payouts are issued monthly. You receive your share of sales on a regular, predictable schedule. Payments are made via Mobile Money, Bank Transfer, or other available methods.

How long does the approval process take?

Most books are reviewed and approved within 24–48 hours. You will receive an email notification once your book is approved and live in the bookstore. If your submission requires changes, you will be contacted with feedback.

What do I need to submit my book?

To submit your book, you will need:

  • Your manuscript in PDF format
  • A cover image (JPG, PNG, or WEBP)
  • Book details: title, subtitle (optional), synopsis, author bio
  • Genre, language, and number of pages
  • Your chosen price (free or paid)
  • 3–5 keywords for discoverability
Can I update my book after it's published?

Yes. You can contact us with updates to your book listing, including price changes, updated manuscript files, or cover image changes. Please email us at reach.volant@gmail.com with your request.

Buying & Reading

How do I purchase a book?

To purchase a book, simply browse the bookstore, select a book, and click the purchase button. You will be guided through the payment process. After payment, you will be able to download your book immediately.

Are there free books available?

Yes! Authors have the option to offer their books for free. You can find free books by browsing the bookstore and looking for books marked as "Free". No payment is required to download free books.

What format are the books in?

All books on Volant Reads are provided as PDF (Portable Document Format) files. This format works on all devices including computers, tablets, and smartphones. You will need a PDF reader app to view your purchased books.

Can I share purchased books with others?

No. Purchased ebooks are for personal use only. Sharing, distributing, or uploading purchased books to any platform is strictly prohibited. This helps protect authors' rights and supports their work.

Account

Do I need an account to buy or download books?

Yes. You need to create an account and sign in to purchase and download books. This helps us keep track of your purchases and ensure secure access to your books. Registration is quick and free.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, visit the Sign Up page, fill in your details, and click "Sign Up". You will need a valid email address and a secure password. Once registered, you can start browsing and purchasing books.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes. We take your privacy seriously. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Payments are processed through secure third-party providers. Please see our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, please contact us at reach.volant@gmail.com with your request. We will process your request and remove your personal data from our systems in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
