Refund Policy

Understanding our refund policy for digital book purchases on Volant Reads.

Bookstore / About / Refund Policy

Overview

At Volant Reads, we strive to provide a seamless and satisfying experience for all readers. Because our products are digital downloads, our refund policy is designed to balance customer satisfaction with the nature of digital content.

Please read this policy carefully before making a purchase. By completing a purchase on Volant Reads, you agree to the terms outlined below.

Digital Products Are Generally Non-Refundable

All books sold on Volant Reads are digital products (PDF ebooks). Due to the nature of digital content, all sales are final once the book has been downloaded.

Important: Refunds are generally not available for digital products after they have been downloaded. Please ensure you are purchasing the correct book before completing your order.

However, we understand that exceptional circumstances may arise. Refunds may be considered in the following cases:

  • Duplicate Purchases — if you accidentally purchased the same book twice.
  • Technical Issues — if you encountered a technical problem that prevented you from accessing or downloading your book.

How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are eligible for a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us — Send an email to reach.volant@gmail.com with the subject line "Refund Request — [Your Order Reference]".
  2. Provide Evidence — Include a clear reason for your refund request and attach any relevant evidence (screenshots, error messages, proof of duplicate purchase, etc.).
  3. Include Your Details — Provide your full name, email address used for the purchase, and order reference or receipt.
Review Time: We aim to review all refund requests within 3–5 business days. You will receive a response via email with our decision.

What We Need From You

To process your refund request efficiently, please ensure your email includes:

  • Full Name — the name used for the purchase.
  • Email Address — the email associated with your Volant Reads account.
  • Order Reference / Receipt — proof of purchase.
  • Reason for Refund — a clear explanation of why you are requesting a refund.
  • Evidence — screenshots or documentation supporting your claim (if applicable).
