How to Submit Your Manuscript

Follow these simple steps to publish your book on Volant Reads.

  1. Create an Account — Sign up on Volant Reads (or log in if you already have an account).
  2. Prepare Your Files — Have your PDF manuscript and cover image ready (JPG, PNG, or WEBP).
  3. Fill in Book Details — Provide title, subtitle, author name, synopsis, genre, language, and number of pages.
  4. Set Your Price — Choose your currency and set your price. You can offer your book for free or sell it.
  5. Add Keywords — Add 3–5 keywords to help readers discover your book.
  6. Submit for Approval — All submissions go through a review process. Approved books appear in the bookstore within 24 hours.
  7. Track Your Sales — Monitor your earnings in real time through your author dashboard.
  8. Promote Your Book — Share your store link or book page link to drive sales and grow your readership.
Approval Time: Most books are reviewed and approved within 24–48 hours. You will be notified via email once your book is live.
