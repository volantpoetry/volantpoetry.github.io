🏆 How Our Ranking System Works

🏆 Poem Ranking

Poems are ranked based on engagement from the last 7 days. Old poems can still win if they receive recent activity!

Score = (Views × 1) + (Likes × 3) + (Comments × 4)
  • 👁️ Views (Reads) ×1 point
  • ❤️ Likes ×3 points
  • 💬 Comments ×4 points
📝 Example:
100 views + 20 likes + 5 comments
= (100×1) + (20×3) + (5×4)
= 100 + 60 + 20 = 180 points

🔄 Rolling 7 Days: Only activity from the last 7 days counts. Poems fall off after 7 days of no activity.

👤 Poet Ranking

Poets are ranked weekly (Monday to Sunday). Winners are announced every Monday!

Score = (Poems × 2) + (Likes Received × 1.5) + (Comments Received × 1.5) + (Likes Given × 3) + (Comments Given × 4)
  • ✍️ Poems Written ×2 points
  • ⭐ Likes Received ×1.5 points
  • 💬 Comments Received ×1.5 points
  • ❤️→ Likes Given to Others ×3 points
  • 💬→ Comments Given to Others ×4 points
Key Insight:
Giving feedback (likes/comments) is worth MORE than receiving it!
This encourages a supportive community where everyone engages with each other.

📅 Weekly Reset: Rankings reset every Monday. The previous week's champion is saved in our Hall of Fame!

💡 Why This System?

Our ranking system is designed to reward genuine community engagement, not just popularity. Commenting thoughtfully on others' work is valued more than receiving likes. This creates a supportive environment where every poet can thrive!

Community Guidelines

Volant Poetry is a creative home for poets and poetry lovers. Share your work, explore new voices, and immerse yourself in a community where every word matters.

❗ Volant Poetry is an open community. Poets can freely share their work without editorial approval. To maintain quality, please follow these simple standards:

  1. All poems must be written by the author themselves.
  2. No AI-generated content is allowed.
  3. Respect other poets and readers, no spam or plagiarism.
  4. Use proper titles and basic formatting.
